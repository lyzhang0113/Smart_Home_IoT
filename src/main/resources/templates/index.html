SmartPot

Many people worldwide have difficulties taking care of plants.
We want to change that.

How does it work?

Smart Pot is a device that will take care of your plants automatically.
Automatic
Watering

The pot will automatically water the plant with pre-set intervals.

Automatic
Lighting

The pot will automatically give light to the plant with pre-set intervals.

Remind to
Fertilize plants

The pot will remind you when to fertilize the plant.

Modular Design

The plant and the reservoir could be separated so that the user could clean the reservoir easily.

Plant Database

SmartPot comes with a built-in database with all kinds of plant data for the user to easily select the perfect default setting for any plant.
